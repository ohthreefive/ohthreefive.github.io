2. 84 year old patient for PTC. Repeated pulsatile ventricular tachycardia pre-procedure and defibrillation from ICD. Once given Amiodarone and Magnesium, his rate settled and he was transferred to coronary care. No further events over next five days. Re-attended for procedure but had three further shocks on table before starting. (0403313155)

The sepsis case highlights the importance of the consent process as per the death discussed above

Both cases brought into light the unfamiliarity many staff have with immediate life support and management of them. We should invite the resus team to give us a tutorial / refresher. A talk on sepsis management would also be useful. MH will try to arrange the latter.
