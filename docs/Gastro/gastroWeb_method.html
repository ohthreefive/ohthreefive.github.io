Martin Hennessy

20/05/2021

  • Included are data about 793 gastrostomies performed on 754 patients between 2014-01-06 and 2021-05-06
  • Repeat gastrostomies are counted only if the new gastrostomy required a new gastric puncture
  • Re-canalisation of existing tracts with a wire are not included
