Radiological gastrostomy - 30 day mortality

Martin Hennessy

29/12/2022

  • Included are data about gastrostomies performed on 899 patients between 2014-01-06 and 2022-12-29

30 day mortality

  • Overall local 30 day mortality after gastrostomy is 5%

  • Combined with 39 series in the literature, 465 patients died within 30 days of gastrostomy out of 5949 patients, giving a mean 30 day mortality of 7.8%

  • Local data are broken down by diagnosis as below:
