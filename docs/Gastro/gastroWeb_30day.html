Radiological gastrostomy - 30 day mortality

Martin Hennessy

2024-02-22

  • Included are data about gastrostomies performed on 1017 patients between 2014-01-06 and 2023-12-21

30 day mortality

  • Overall local 30 day mortality after gastrostomy is 5%

  • Combined with 40 series in the literature, 476 patients died within 30 days of gastrostomy out of 6549 patients, giving a mean 30 day mortality of 7.3%

  • Local data are broken down by diagnosis as below:
